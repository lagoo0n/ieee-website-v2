About Us

IEEE at Cal Poly SLO is the student branch of the global Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. We support professional, academic, and social growth through industry talks, skill-building workshops, and community events that enhance the engineering experience at Cal Poly.

Each year, we run hands-on technical projects led by experienced student mentors, giving members the chance to build practical skills, explore new technologies, and tackle real engineering challenges. Whether you want to learn, create, or connect, IEEE at Cal Poly offers opportunities for every aspiring engineer.
